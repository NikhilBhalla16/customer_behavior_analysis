
# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using a retail shopping dataset containing **3,900 transactions** and **18 customer and purchase-related attributes**. The goal was to uncover actionable business insights related to customer demographics, spending habits, subscriptions, discounts, product preferences, and shipping choices.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow including:

- Data Cleaning & Preparation using Python
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based business analysis in PostgreSQL
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard Development
- Business Reporting & Presentation Creation

The findings provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention, increase revenue, and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large amounts of customer transaction data but often struggle to translate it into actionable insights.

This project aims to answer questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
- How do subscriptions impact spending behavior?
- Are discount users more valuable customers?
- Which products receive the highest ratings?
- How does shipping preference influence purchase value?

## Key Insights

### Revenue by Gender

Female customers generated slightly higher overall revenue compared to male customers, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.

### Subscription Impact

Subscribers demonstrated significantly stronger purchasing behavior:

- **68% higher spending**
- **45% of total revenue contribution**
- **78% loyalty rate**

This indicates that subscription programs are a major driver of customer value.

### Discount Usage Analysis

High-spending customers actively utilized discounts, indicating that promotions are not only attracting price-sensitive buyers but also retaining premium customers.

### Shipping Preferences

Customers selecting Express Shipping spent approximately **12% more per transaction** than Standard Shipping users.

### Top-Rated Products

Products receiving the highest customer satisfaction scores included:

- Blouse
- Dress
- Shirt

These products present strong opportunities for promotional campaigns and inventory prioritization.

### Customer Segmentation

| Segment | Percentage |
|----------|----------|
| New Customers | 50% |
| Returning Customers | 35% |
| Loyal Customers | 15% |

A significant opportunity exists to convert new customers into repeat buyers through loyalty and retention strategies.
